Welcome to
Tampa Bay Thrives

Tampa Bay Thrives is a unique convening of community leaders within the Tampa Bay area who understand the importance of improving access to resources for mental health and substance use issues. Tampa Bay Thrives' immediate priorities are improving behavioral health navigation, access, and awareness to the communities of Hillsborough, Pasco, Pinellas, and Polk counties.

Founded in 2019, Tampa Bay Thrives was formed to improve the lives of individuals greatly affected by mental health and substance use disorders. Discover more about Tampa Bay Thrives.

Learn More

A Mental Wellness
Coallition

Our Mission is to mobilize the community to strengthen behavioral health outcomes for depression, anxiety and substance use disorder, with a focus on improving early intervention, access and awareness. Learn more about our purpose in the Tampa Bay area.

Our first major initiative, Let's Talk, is a Hillsborough County pilot providing free, confidential, 24/7 behavioral support navigation support line for individuals experiencing mild to moderate mental health or substance use issues. Learn more about Let's Talk.
